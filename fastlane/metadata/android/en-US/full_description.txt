Aura Music is an open-source music player that lets you stream music directly from YouTube Music.

Features:
- Stream music from YouTube Music
- Video playback support
- Lyrics display and translation
- Audio quality settings
- Background playback
- Queue management
- Search functionality
- Artist and album browsing

This is the FOSS (Free and Open Source) version, compatible with F-Droid. It does not include Google Play Services dependencies.
